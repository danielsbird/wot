About

What's Out There Mohawk is a series of free talks by software professionals to help students start their careers in the information technology industry. Many of our speakers are Mohawk College graduates or representatives from companies that employ Mohawk College students.

Our events give students an opportunity to meet employers and learn how to get a great co-op placement or job when they graduate.

What's Out There Mohawk is organized by students enrolled in Mohawk College's Computer Systems Technology program. Please contact us if you are a student or employer who wants to be involved in our events.
